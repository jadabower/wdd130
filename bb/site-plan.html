Overview

Purpose

Our goal at Beautifully Blocky is to take your ideas and turn them into beautiful builds for you to implement in your Minecraft world. Just provide us with as much information as you want, whether it be just the seed and location, or a full color palate and build style. Our team of professional builders will work with you personally to create the perfect build to brighten your Minecraft world, one block at a time.

Audience

Minecraft players who have a place to build something and need something beautiful to put there.

Content

Home page

The Home page should include a description of the service that the website provides. It should be something like this: Sometimes, as a Minecraft player, it can be difficult to bring to life the grand ideas you have for a build. Especially when you have the absolute perfect spot for something, and don't want to waste a perfect opportunity on a dirt house. That's why we here at Beautifully Blocky decided that this building process should not be stressful, and we are here to help you with all your builds. Just click "Get Building" above and fill out the form describing your dream build. Our builders will work on a build specifically for your world, and they will respond in 5 to 7 business days with pictures of the build as well as a list of resources the build requires. This service is completely free. Check out the "Build Gallery" page to see some of the builds we have done for users like you. Also be sure to follow us on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Contact us through beautifully_blocky@fakemail.com or call (415) 523-0057. Thank you for coming to us for all your building needs. Go build something beautiful!

Get Building

The Get Building page should include a form with several input boxes like "World Seed", "X-Coordinate", "Y-Coordinate", and "Z-Coordinate". It should also have a box to put a description of what build style/size/type of building they want, any restrictions of materials they have, and a color or build palate. They could also choose between whether they want a fully complete build or just a structure with no details or something like that. They can also input either their phone number or email so that we can send the finished product.
